Zackary Longoria;The life of the young one

Life started out difficult in 1995 when I was born. I opened my eyes to begin my life and slowly realized what an adventure this life would truly be. The first three years or so were fairly enjoyable. My father and mother mostly got along and I just played with toys and outside. Then life began to be a true journey. I remember my parents arguing and fighting constantly when I was three. They split, violently, and left me torn. I lived with my father for the rest of my fifteen years of life with visitation to my mother. Again, the first few years were great, until she got into drugs. Life began to be a series of events that left me not knowing where I was going to be sleeping, eating, or pretty much anything. After she left my step-father, when I was 15, life began to finally be stable.

I met my wife at the young age of fifteen as well. We started dating and ended up getting married when I was twenty. We started our family at the age of twenty-one, and have two children now, four and one, with a third on the way. Mostly our hobbies at the moment include playing outside, shooting guns or bows, and riding off road vehicles. Now we just live life day by day, and enjoy our children.
