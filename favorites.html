Peaky Blinders
An old gangster show. The main character starts out as a horse bookie taking bets. He then procedes to expand his business throughout the show into all sorts of things, including a contract with the king, liqour exportation, and wall street investing. First aired in 2013 and ended in 2022.
Seven Deadly Sins
A young princess gets saved by a random child with a dragon tatoo on his arm. He later becomes known as Meliodas, the dragon sin of wrath. He proceeds to get his group of misfits back together and save the kingdom from corruption within itself. The protectors of the realm turned evil. The first episode aired in October 2014 and the last episode aired in 2021. There is still a few movies promised without a date yet.
Jujutsu no kaisen
Another japanase anime that first aired in 2020 and is currently releasing a prequel movie and a season 2. Its about a young boy who learns about curses, or demons, and gets thrown into fighting them. He accidently on purpose swallows a cursed object, a sealed spirit of a powerful curse, that infuses him with the curses power. The curse he bonds with is the strongest, oldest, and most evil curse. His journey takes him to a school of people similiar to him so he doesn't get killed.
Vampire Dairies
First aired in in 2009 and sadly the last episode was in 2017. There are spin offs that continue and interfere with the storyline as well. The story follows a young girl who's a doppleganger and two vampires who both loved her previous doppleganger. She eventually turns into a vampire. All her friends, including her brother, end up having some sort of super natural ability. The show involves witches, nature, balance, vampires, wolves, hybrids and so on.
The Sandman
The first episode aired just recently, August 5, 2022 and is set for a season two. It follows an entity of the Endless, Dream. Also known as the sandman he controls the realm of dreams and nightmares. One of the nightmares he created, The Corinthian, escapes the dream realm and begins a seriel killing spree. The Corinthian runs from Dream for centuries after Dream gets captured.
